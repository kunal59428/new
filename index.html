ABOUT US

Humparivar.com (Lakhithvi Online Services (OPC) Private Limited) is India’s startup online grocery store. With over 2500 plus products and over 500+ brands in our catalogue you will find everything you are looking for. Right from Floor, Rice, Pulses, Spices and Seasonings Packaged products, Beverages, Personal care, Cosmetics, etc. We have it all. Choose from a wide range of options in every category, exclusively handpicked to help you find the best quality available. Select products and your order will be delivered right to your doorstep, anywhere in Delhi, Noida, Greater Noida, Gurugram, Faridabad & Ghaziabad. You can pay online using your debit/credit card, Paytm, other Wallets, UPI and Net Banking etc. We believe in creating lasting experiences and go the extra mile to serve our customers better. To make their journey an enjoyable one, our knowledgeable and passionate staff answers any queries they may have, help one put together a perfect fine grocery for a special occasion. We guarantee on-time delivery and the best quality!
